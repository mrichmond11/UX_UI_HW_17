Im Marli

I'm a DC-based visual designer & illustrator specializing in user interface and experience design. I enjoy creating engaging experiences, systems, and solutions that empower people & communities.

I am continually inspired by the lived experiences & motivations of real people and rely on a holistic design approach that is guided by research. I use clean, minimalist design to craft unique and responsive experiences for web and mobile and often convey my ideas through illustration and prototyping.
